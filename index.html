About me

Hello! My name is Purest Sage, a blue, sleepy pony and the owner of this site. I created this site as an archive page for all of my projects and also a place to get support for my production designs.

I'm mostly a programmer by trade but work on electronics in my spare time. My interest is in vintage electronics such as logic ICs, Nixie tubes, and relay circuits, but I also work with new designs such as microcontrollers and networking. Due to time and money constraints, I still have only a few finished (and a lot of abandoned or in-progress) projects. Someday I might be able to create more.
